## Part 1: The Vision (5 minutes)

### What We're Building

**SpaceGraphJS is the first self-building UI framework.**

It sees what it builds, verifies quality autonomously, and self-corrects in 30 seconds.

**98% faster than traditional AI iteration. Pure FOSS. Industry-defining.**

---

### The Problem We Solve

**AI-assisted development has a fundamental flaw:**

The AI cannot see what it builds.

```
Traditional AI Development:
Human describes → AI codes → Human views → Human describes → AI guesses → Repeat (10-20x)
                                ↓
                        30 minutes per iteration
                        Exhausting. Imprecise. Discouraging.
```

---

### Our Solution

**Vision-Closed Development:**

```
Vision-Closed Development:
Human specifies → AI builds → Vision verifies → AI self-corrects → Done
                                ↓
                        30 seconds per iteration
                        Autonomous. Precise. Empowering.
```

**6 AI Vision Models:**
| Model | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **LQ-Net** | Layout quality scoring |
| **TLA** | Text legibility analysis |
| **CHE** | Color harmony evaluation |
| **ODN** | Overlap detection |
| **VHS** | Visual hierarchy scoring |
| **EQA** | Ergonomics quality assessment |

### Buffers & Resilience

| Level         | Buffer          | Purpose                               |
| ------------- | --------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Task**      | +50% time       | Daily overruns, unexpected complexity |
| **Week**      | 30% uncommitted | Sick days, meetings, fatigue          |
| **Milestone** | 1 slip allowed  | Complex tasks, external dependencies  |
| **Phase**     | 2-4 weeks       | Major setbacks, pivots, rest          |

**No-Guilt Delay Policy:**

1. Acknowledge: "This is taking longer than expected"
2. Investigate: "Why? What's the blocker?"
3. Adjust: "What's the new realistic date?"
4. Communicate: "Here's the updated timeline"
5. Continue: "Moving forward with adjusted plan"
